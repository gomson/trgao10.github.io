Week 1 Lecture Videos (Prof. Ingrid Daubechies)

Published: Tue 01 September 2015
Updated: Tue 01 September 2015
By Ingrid Daubechies

In Math 212.

tags: Math 212 Videos

Professor Ingrid Daubechies is a James B. Duke Professor of Mathematics at Duke University. She has taught MATH 212 many times, and served as the course coordinater in Academic Year 2012-2013. During Fall 2014, she recorded a series of course lecture videos for her flipped classroom experiment. These lecture videos are all featured by Prof. Daubechies herself, covering all major topics from vectors to vector calculus in the MATH 212 syllabus. Each of these videos comprises a thorough walk-through of her lecture slides, several 3D data visualization in MATLAB, or a mixture of both. Some of these videos can be found on the joint MATH 212 Sakai page.

Part 1/2: Vectors, Addition/Subtraction, Scalar Multiplication, Lines in \(\mathbb{R}^3\)

Part 2/2: Dot Product, Cross Product, Scalar Triple Product, Planes in \(\mathbb{R}^3\)
