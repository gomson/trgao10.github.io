Paraboloid

$$x^2+y^2-z=0$$

parametric equaitons: for \(t\in\left[0,\infty\right)\), \(\theta\in\left[0,2\pi\right]\),

\begin{equation*} \left\{ \begin{array}{rl} x &= t\cos\theta,\\ y &= t\sin\theta,\\ z &= t. \end{array} \right. \end{equation*}

Hyperboloid (One-Sheet)

$$x^2+y^2-z^2=1$$

parametric equaitons: for \(t\in\left(-\infty,\infty\right)\), \(\theta\in\left[0,2\pi\right]\),

\begin{equation*} \left\{ \begin{array}{rl} x &= \cosh t\cos\theta,\\ y &= \cosh t\sin\theta,\\ z &= \sinh t. \end{array} \right. \end{equation*}

Hyperboloid (Two-Sheets)

$$x^2+y^2-z^2=-1$$

parametric equaitons: for \(t\in\left[0,\infty\right)\), \(\theta\in\left[0,2\pi\right]\),

\begin{equation*} \left\{ \begin{array}{rl} x &= \sinh t\cos\theta,\\ y &= \sinh t\sin\theta,\\ z &= \pm\cosh t. \end{array} \right. \end{equation*}
